Billing FAQ
When does my trial end?
Your free trial lasts 14 days from when you created your account. You'll see the countdown above.
Do I need a credit card for the trial?
No. Your trial is completely free with no card required. We'll only ask for payment details when you're ready to subscribe.
Can I cancel any time?
Yes — cancel any time from this page. Your access continues until the end of the billing period.
What happens to my data if I cancel?
Your data is retained for 30 days after cancellation. You can export your audit log and ticket history at any time.
