FAQ

Common questions

Do I need to know Active Directory? +

No — that's the whole point. You describe what you need in plain English and the assistant handles the AD side. No PowerShell, no MMC consoles, no jargon.

Is my Active Directory exposed to the internet? +

Never. A lightweight Windows agent polls outbound over HTTPS. No inbound ports, no VPN, no firewall rules. Your AD never faces the internet.

Can the AI do something destructive by mistake? +

A hard permission layer in Python classifies every action. High-blast operations (disabling accounts, bulk OU moves) always require explicit confirmation — the AI cannot bypass it.

Can my whole team use it? +

Yes. Add dashboard users with role-based access — admin (full control) or viewer (read-only). While we're in early access, team seats are unlimited.
