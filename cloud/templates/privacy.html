Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

AID Helpdesk ("we", "us") operates the AID Helpdesk service at web-production-01ecc.up.railway.app. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we protect it.

1. What we collect

  • Account data — company name, email address, and hashed password when you sign up.
  • Active Directory metadata — usernames, display names, organisational units, group names, and account status returned by your Windows agent. We never store AD passwords.
  • Ticket content — support request text submitted by your staff via the ticket form or email.
  • Audit log — a record of every AD action taken (who requested it, what was done, when).
  • Usage metrics — counts of AI scans and AD actions per month for quota tracking.
  • Feedback — voluntary feedback you submit via the in-product feedback button.
  • Technical logs — standard server access logs (IP address, request path, timestamp) for security and debugging.

2. What we do NOT collect

  • AD user passwords (never transmitted to our servers)
  • Payment card details (Stripe handles these; we only store subscription state)
  • Email content beyond what staff submit as support tickets

3. How we use your data

  • To operate the service — process tickets, execute AD commands via your Windows agent, generate reports.
  • To enforce plan quotas and prevent abuse.
  • To send transactional emails (password resets, ticket resolutions, scheduled reports) that you have configured.
  • To improve the product — aggregate, anonymised usage metrics only.

4. Data storage and security

Data is stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted on Railway (Frankfurt, EU / US region depending on your deployment). Data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS. Passwords are hashed with bcrypt. We apply the principle of least privilege — no employee or contractor has routine access to your AD metadata.

Your Active Directory is never directly accessible from the internet. The AID Windows Agent polls outbound over HTTPS; no inbound ports need to be opened on your network.

5. Data retention

Your data is retained for the lifetime of your account. If you close your account, data is deleted within 30 days. You can export your audit log and ticket history at any time from the dashboard.

6. Third parties

  • Anthropic — AI responses are generated using the Claude API. Ticket text and AD metadata may be sent to Anthropic's API for processing. Anthropic's privacy policy applies.
  • Railway — our hosting provider. Data is stored on Railway's infrastructure.
  • Stripe — payment processing (when paid plans are available). Card data goes directly to Stripe.
  • Mailgun / SendGrid / Postmark — optional email delivery providers you may configure for email ticket intake.

We do not sell your data to any third party.

7. Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data at any time by emailing lachyswebdev@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Cookies

We use a single session cookie to keep you logged in to the dashboard. No third-party tracking or advertising cookies are used.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be notified by email to the admin address on your account.

10. Contact

Questions or concerns: lachyswebdev@gmail.com
